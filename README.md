## 📖 Introduction

**DiTing System** is an integrated quality management and retrospective system inspired by Chinese mythology.

**DiTing (谛听)** is the mount of Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva in Chinese Buddhism, known for its ability to:
- ✅ **Distinguish truth from falsehood** - Quality detection
- ✅ **Perceive everything** - Comprehensive monitoring
- ✅ **Discern good from evil** - Identify normal vs. abnormal

## 🎯 Best Practices

### 1. Checklist-Based Work

- ✅ Always use checklists
- ✅ Never rely on memory
- ✅ Verify each item

### 2. Role-by-Role Review

- ✅ Review each role systematically
- ✅ Don't skip any role
- ✅ Ensure equal attention

### 3. Data Integrity

- ✅ Read data in chunks (200 rows/block)
- ✅ Check truncated flag
- ✅ Verify completeness

### 4. Cross-Validation

- ✅ Self-check after completion
- ✅ Cross-check with others
- ✅ Verify before submission

## 📊 Case Studies

### CASE-2026-04-08-001: Weekly Summary Missing Art Team

**Problem**: Art team work was omitted from weekly summary

**Root Cause**:
- No checklist-based workflow
- Relied on impression-based summary
- Data truncation not detected

**Solution**:
- Created standardized workflow
- Implemented chunk-based reading
- Added completeness verification

**Lessons**:
- Always use checklists
- Read data in chunks
- Verify completeness
